Repository URL Formatting

Format: (optional items are in (), replace properties in {}) Example:
(https://)github.com/{user}/{repository} https://github.com/pageletdev/example-pagelets
(https://)github.com/{user}/{repository}/blob/master/{path/to/file} https://github.com/pageletdev/example-pagelets/blob/master/index.html
(https://)raw.githubusercontent.com/{user}/{repository}/master/{path/to/file} https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pageletdev/example-pagelets/master/index.html
(https://)github.com/{user}/{repository}/tree/{version} https://github.com/pageletdev/example-pagelets/tree/641397bf53fc34306a0d6d34386e643542425a47
(https://)github.com/{user}/{repository}/blob/{version}/{path/to/file} https://github.com/pageletdev/example-pagelets/blob/641397bf53fc34306a0d6d34386e643542425a47/index.html
(https://)raw.githubusercontent.com/{user}/{repository}/{version}/{path/to/file} raw.githubusercontent.com/pageletdev/example-pagelets/641397bf53fc34306a0d6d34386e643542425a47/index.html
